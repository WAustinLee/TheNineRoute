About

The Nine Route is a weekly podcast that airs live on Wednesday nights at 9pm PT on Google Hangouts and YouTube.

Austin Lee and Alex Miglio crunch the numbers, study the tape, and track the news to help you win. Rankings, sleepers, streaming options, day-gaming targets, obscure movie references, bad puns, and more!

Use the Twitter hashtag #9Route to submit questions that we will answer on-air.
